Company

We're building the bridge to the physical world.

Intelligence is becoming abundant. The economy still assumes a human has to be in the loop for every real-world act. Provenant removes that assumption — safely.

The bet

As agents absorb more of the paperwork economy, the bottleneck stops being intelligence and becomes the handful of steps that must touch the legal and physical world: an identity verified, a document notarized, a site inspected. Those steps can't be reasoned away — they need a real act with a result you can trust. Whoever gives agents a verifiable way to perform them becomes critical infrastructure for the agentic economy.

So we're building one API for real-world actions, each returning a cryptographically verifiable proof. We start with identity and notarization — high-frequency, legally required, and structured — and grow the set of verbs from there.

Principles

  • Verifiable over trusted. Every result is a signed proof anyone can check, not a promise.
  • Agent-first. An endpoint and a webhook, not a web app a human clicks through.
  • Compliance is the product. The regulatory work is the moat, not a cost center.
  • Real fulfillment. We integrate licensed providers; we don't fake the act.
